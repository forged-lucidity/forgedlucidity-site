ELICIT — Cross-Domain Coherence Validation

The empirical research program that tests the framework against real data
Seven of nine domains holding · Spectral exponent analysis · Cardiovascular applications · CITI certified · IRB-ready

ELICIT detects coherence signatures across independent substrates — neural, oceanic, atmospheric, seismic, and solar among them — using established signal processing methods. Of nine domains tested the predicted direction held in seven; we report the two reversals (financial among them) and recalibrate every p-value against spectrum-matched nulls before headlining a figure. The cross-domain replication strategy serves as the primary robustness check: a coherence signature that appears across mechanistically independent domains cannot be explained by single-domain artifact. Clinical application focuses on cardiovascular digital twin validation and early coherence degradation detection.
